<p>Congratulations on your posting to our school! We look forward to welcoming
your child/ ward to our school community.</p>

<p><strong>Date:</strong> 22 December 2025 (Monday)</p>
<p><strong>Time:</strong> 09:00 - 10:00 AM</p>

</p>

about your child's/ ward's transition to secondary school.</p>
