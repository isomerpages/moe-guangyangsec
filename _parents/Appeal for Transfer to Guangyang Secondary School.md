<h3><strong>Who Can Apply?</strong></h3>
<p>Primary 6 students who have just received their secondary school posting
in 2025 may submit appeal for transfer to Guangyang Secondary School. Transfer
appeals are subject to available vacancies and the school’s admission criteria.
The school will not disclose reasons for selection or non-selection.</p>
<h4><strong>Important Notes</strong></h4>
<ul>
<li>
<p><strong>Cut-off Point</strong>: Your child's PSLE score must meet the
school's cut-off point of the posting year.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>DSA-Sec Students:</strong>&nbsp;Students allocated via Direct
School Admission – Secondary are not eligible for transfer as they should
honour their commitment to their allocated school.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Reporting to Posted School</strong>: Your child should still report
to the posted school while the appeal is being processed.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<h3><strong>Key Dates</strong></h3>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p><strong>Submission Deadline:</strong>&nbsp;26 December 2025, 6:00pm</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Appeal Outcome:</strong>&nbsp;The application is deemed to be
unsuccessful if you do not receive an offer from the school by 9 January
2026.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<h3><strong>Before You Begin</strong></h3>
<p>Please have the following ready:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p><strong>PSLE result slip</strong> (soft copy for upload)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Reason for appeal</strong> (300 characters)</p>
</li>
</ul>
