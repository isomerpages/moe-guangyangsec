<h3><strong>Who Can Apply?</strong></h3>
<p>Primary 6 students who have just received their secondary school posting
in 2025 may use this form to appeal for transfer to Guangyang Secondary
School. Your child's PSLE score must meet the school's cut-off point of
the posting year. Transfer will be subject to available vacancies and the
schoolâ€™s admission criteria.</p>
<h4><strong>Important Notes</strong></h4>
<ul>
<li>
<p><strong>Transferring OUT of GYSS?</strong> If your child was posted to
GYSS and you wish to transfer out, please visit your preferred school's
website to submit their appeal form.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>DSA-Sec Students:</strong> Students allocated via DSA-Sec cannot
transfer and must honour their commitment to their allocated school.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Medical Appeals:</strong> For appeals based on serious medical
conditions or severe physical impairments, submit your request through
your child's posted school by 12pm on the next working day after posting
results are released.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<h3><strong>Key Dates</strong></h3>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p><strong>Deadline:</strong> 26 December 2025, 6:00pm (late submissions will
not be accepted.)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Report to Posted School:</strong> 2 January 2026 (your child must
report to their posted school while the appeal is being processed)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Appeal Outcome:</strong> If you do not receive a response by 9
January 2026, your application is unsuccessful.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<h3><strong>Before You Begin</strong></h3>
<p>Please have the following ready:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p><strong>PSLE result slip</strong> (soft copy for upload)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Reason for appeal</strong> (300 characters)</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>Transfer is subject to available vacancies and admission criteria. Schools
will not disclose reasons for selection or non-selection. Personal information
will be used solely for processing this appeal.</p>
