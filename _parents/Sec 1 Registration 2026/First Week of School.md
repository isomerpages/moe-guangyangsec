<h3><strong>First Day of School</strong></h3>
<p><strong>Date:</strong> Friday, 2 January 2026</p>
<p><strong>Reporting Details:</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p><strong>Assembly Time:</strong> 7.20am</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Venue:</strong> School Hall (Student leaders will be present to
guide students)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Dismissal Time:</strong> 12.30pm</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Attire:</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Full school uniform</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>White canvas shoes or plain-coloured track shoes (black or dark-coloured)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Plain white socks (visible socks must be plain white only)</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Grooming:</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Boys: Hair must be sloped at the sides and back</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Girls: Hair must be neatly tied</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>What to Bring:</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Water bottle</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Writing materials</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>A story book</p>
</li>
</ul>
