<h4><strong>Guangyang Alumni — Where Friendships Last a Lifetime</strong></h4>
<p>At the heart of the Guangyang Alumni is a simple but powerful belief:
that the bonds formed within our school walls don't end at graduation.
We are a community of graduates — past and present — united by shared memories,
a love for our alma mater, and a desire to give back to the generations
that follow.</p>
<p>Through our events and gatherings, we create spaces where old friendships
are rekindled, new ones are forged, and our students get to learn from
the rich experiences of those who have walked the same corridors before
them.</p>
<h4><strong>Reliving the Moments</strong></h4>
<p>In 2023, we celebrated a truly special milestone — our 105th year Homecoming
Lunch — welcoming graduates back to the place where it all began, and reminding
us all that no matter how much time passes, Guangyang will always feel
like home. In 2024, alumni members came together during the Mid-Autumn
Festival, sharing laughter and creating new memories to treasure. In 2025,
our alumni generously returned to share their life experiences and showcase
their crafts, inspiring the next generation and strengthening the ties
that bind our community together.</p>
